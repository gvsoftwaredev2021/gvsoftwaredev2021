Retro 1: Nick Jones

Date Submitted: 2021-9-10

The lifecycle for software developement isn't something that is stable or even predictable to an extent. The lifecycle changes per project you're working on, what company its for, and how they will use it; It's an ever changing process that simultaneous can be some of the most creative work you will ever do while also being some of the most monotinous and tedious work you will ever do. For that reason I could probably see myself doing it consistantly, despite the low lows of tediously entering in lines of code that I've trial and error'd for the 50th time, the high highs allow me to show a creative process through a medium that I am passionate about.
